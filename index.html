Selected Engineering Projects

ONUK STC-20E Aerodynamics Development

Aerodynamics CFD SimScale CAD

Carried out a full computational fluid dynamics (CFD) study of the Onuk STC-20E sports car to evaluate external aerodynamics. Developed the car's underbody by designing a splitter and rear diffuser, optimizing high-speed downforce. Conducted simulations using a steady-state incompressible RANS solver with the k-w SST turbulence model, quantifying drag, downforce coefficients, and Center of Pressure (CoP) migration.

Dartmouth Formula Racing (FSAE)

Vehicle Dynamics Aerodynamics Composites Manufacturing COMSOL

As the Aerodynamics Lead, I developed a comprehensive aero package including front and rear wings, an undertray, and sidepods designed to maximize low-speed cornering grip. Beyond aerodynamic design and COMSOL/SimScale simulations, I took full responsibility for composites manufacturing, including designing molds, executing carbon fiber layups, and vacuum bagging lightweight bodywork.

Autonomous UAV Platforms (Balaban & Martı)

Airframe Design Structural Engineering Control Systems

Contributed to the design and development of multiple UAV platforms. The Balaban is an autonomous platform designed for long-range (40+ km) missions, achieving a top speed of 138 km/h utilizing a CNC laser-cut birch skeletal frame. The Martı is a coastal search and rescue UAV featuring a highly modular, low-cost foam composite mainframe designed to withstand harsh, high-moisture saltwater environments.

Patented Mechanical & Propulsion Systems

Mechanical Design Propulsion Thermodynamics

Inventor on multiple patent applications submitted to the Turkish Patent Institute, focusing on unconventional mechanical and propulsion systems:

  • Rotary Expansion ICE (TR202103765A2): An internal combustion engine design placing pistons on a hexagonal geometry to maximize effective surface area during the combustion cycle.
  • Nitinol Energy Recovery Mechanism (TR2021013055): A high-torque mechanism recovering energy from high-temperature water using shape-memory Nickel-Titanium alloys.
  • Piezo-Electrostatic Droplet Propulsion (TR2022001490): A conceptual small-scale electric propulsion system utilizing vibrating mesh grids and a high potential difference to accelerate ionized propellant.
